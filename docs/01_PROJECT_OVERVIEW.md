# English Galaxy Academy

## Project Vision

English Galaxy Academy is a comprehensive English learning platform designed to support learners from primary school to high school according to the Turkish Ministry of National Education (MEB) English curriculum, while also providing a dedicated General English learning environment for independent learners.

The platform aims to become a centralized digital resource hub where students, teachers, parents, and lifelong learners can access high-quality English learning materials organized by grade level, skill area, and proficiency level.

The system will use a Markdown-based content architecture to ensure scalability, maintainability, and efficient content production.

---
