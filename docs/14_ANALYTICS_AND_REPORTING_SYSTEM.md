# English Galaxy Academy

## Analytics and Reporting System

The platform must provide comprehensive analytics and reporting capabilities for administrators, content managers, teachers, and future institutional partners.

Analytics should support educational decision-making, content optimization, curriculum coverage analysis, and platform growth.

---

# Analytics Philosophy

Data should be used to improve:

* Learning outcomes
* Content quality
* Curriculum coverage
* User experience
* Platform growth

Analytics must be actionable.
