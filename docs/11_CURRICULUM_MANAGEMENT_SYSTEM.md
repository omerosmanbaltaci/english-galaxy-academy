# English Galaxy Academy

## Curriculum Management System

The Curriculum Management System (CMS) serves as the educational foundation of the platform.

All educational content must be linked to curriculum structures rather than manually organized content categories.

The curriculum system should support:

* MEB Curriculum
* Cambridge Curriculum
* Oxford Curriculum
* Custom Learning Paths
* Future International Frameworks

---

# Curriculum Philosophy

The curriculum is the source of truth.

Content should never exist independently.

Every lesson, worksheet, quiz, flashcard, and teacher resource must be mapped to curriculum data.
