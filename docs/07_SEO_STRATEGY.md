# English Galaxy Academy

## SEO Strategy

The platform must be designed with SEO as a core architectural requirement.

The objective is to achieve strong visibility across search engines, educational searches, and AI-powered search experiences.

---

# SEO Goals

Primary Goals:

* Increase organic traffic
* Improve content discoverability
* Rank for curriculum-based searches
* Rank for General English searches
* Support AI search indexing
