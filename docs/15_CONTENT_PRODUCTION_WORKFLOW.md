# English Galaxy Academy

## Content Production Workflow

This document defines the complete lifecycle of educational content from planning to publication.

The workflow ensures consistency, quality, curriculum alignment, and scalability.

---

# Content Production Philosophy

Every resource must:

* Serve a learning objective
* Align with curriculum requirements
* Follow platform standards
* Be reusable
* Be searchable
* Be maintainable
