Frequently Asked Questions

Is English Galaxy Academy aligned with the MEB curriculum?

Yes. All school level resources (Grades 1-12) are designed to support unit titles and learning objectives published by the Turkish Ministry of National Education (MEB).

Are resources printable?

Many worksheets and lesson packs contain dedicated PDF links so teachers and parents can download and print them directly for classroom use.

How does the AI Content Generation work?

Our backend system integrates with LLM APIs utilizing standardized prompts mapping to the exact MEB curriculum outcomes. AI-generated drafts are reviewed and approved by expert editors before publication.
